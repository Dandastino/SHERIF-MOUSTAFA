ABOUT ME


Introduction

My name is Sherif Moustafa, and I was born in Egypt in 2004. I speak Italian, which is my mother tongue, English at a standard level, and Arabic, my native language. Additionally, I enjoy going to the gym to improve my physical fitness. In the past, I played football for two years, an experience that taught me a lot about discipline and teamwork. I hold a diploma as a computer expert, which has equipped me with solid skills in the field of technology.

Who I Am

I am a dynamic person, always seeking new experiences and motivated by a variety of passions that enrich my life every day. Sports are one of my main sources of energy and motivation. Soccer, in particular, is a great passion that I consistently follow, both as a spectator and as a player. Moreover, the gym is a daily challenge for me, offering an opportunity to train not only my body but also my mind, testing my endurance, determination, and ability to improve. I thrive on staying active, and I love walking and engaging in constant physical activity as a way to maintain balance and vitality.

Hobbies

Cooking

I enjoy preparing dishes, experimenting with new ingredients and recipes, and finding in every meal an opportunity to express my creativity. I cook not only for myself but also for others, as sharing a good meal is one of the simplest and most satisfying joys in life. Additionally, energy drinks are part of my routine, helping me maintain high energy levels during intense activities or busy days.

Photography

Photography is another great passion of mine, especially portraits. I love capturing people's expressions and emotions through the lens, aiming to tell a story with each shot. Photography, for me, is a way to freeze time and see the world in a new light. I am also learning to make the most of software like Photoshop and Premiere to enhance my skills in editing photos and videos. This passion drives me to refine every aspect of visual creation, making each image or video a unique and personal narrative.

Intellectual Interests

Programming is another one of my key interests. I find it to be an intellectually stimulating activity that allows me to combine logic and creativity in a productive way. Each new project presents a challenge that pushes me to continually improve and explore new horizons in computer science. My desire for learning extends beyond programming to areas such as finance, psychology, philosophy, and personal growth. These subjects fascinate me because they help me better understand the world, its dynamics, and myself, providing valuable tools for personal development.

Looking Forward

Despite my many passions, I have not yet found a definitive answer to what I want to become. However, I believe that living in the present with enthusiasm and a desire to learn is the best way to build my future. Every experience, every step I take, brings me closer to a deeper understanding of myself and the world around me, and this is the true wealth I aim to cultivate.
